Welcome

Welcome to A4tab.net! Here you'll find guitar tabs and chords which are optimized for printing on one side of A4 (aka letter) paper. What's so great about that? Well:

  • Great for singalongs and jams - you don't need to turn the page halfway through a song
  • All our tabs have ALL the lyrics for the songs - again, great for singalongs
  • More readable (vs printing in 2-up mode)

Our tabs are also good for viewing on an ebook reader like a Kindle.

To print, all you need to do is just go File -> Print in your browser and the tabs should print nicely without any headings or guff.

Browse all A4tabs!

Tabs can also be grouped with other tabs into collections we call Songbooks.
